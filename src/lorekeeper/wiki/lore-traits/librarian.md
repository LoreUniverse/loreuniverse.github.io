Lorekeeper-type

Obtained from the {mechanics|lore-essence-mythos|Mythos} known as the Library Card. This Lore Trait is possessed by {characters|pinelopi|Pinelopi}, the Worldweaver.

At Tale-level, it had the ability to open a portal to a random {mechanics|loreworlds|Loreworld} in the universe. This ability was on a 1-year cooldown, and was one-way only. As soon as someone walked through the portal, the portal would destabilise, staying open for just another half minute before closing forever.

At Divine-level, Pinelopi is able to open portals to anywhere in the universe at will. As long as She knows the story of a location, she can go there.

## Background
The [[Library Card]] was a Mythos that was formed on Earth.
