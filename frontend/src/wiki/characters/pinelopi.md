---
name: Pinelopi
---

Also known as the Worldweaver, she is the first modern Loreseeker on Earth. Her story is detailed in [[Mythos Corp]].

She escaped her imprisonment with Leo and Angie, and took control of the Library Card Mythos. The Library Card wrote the Earth into the Universal Lore and awakened and amplified the dormant Lore power that had accumulated for centuries.

Her current whereabouts are unknown, but she is presumed to be exploring the universe. While not officially part of Leo's group, as Leo's first companion she is welcomed within their ranks.
